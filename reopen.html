Phase

Phase 1

Red Zone
Objective
Flatten the curve and contain the virus as quickly as possible
Guidelines for Individuals
1. All vulnerable individuals remain sheltered in place.
2. Individuals in public should maximize physical distance.
3. Abstaining from social gatherings of more than 10 people.
4. Nonessential travel should remain at a minimum.

Phase 2

Orange Zone
Objective
Cautiously reopening businesses and lifting lockdown
Guidelines for Individuals
1. All vulnerable individuals remain sheltered in place
2. Visits to hospitals and senior living facilities are still not recommended
3. Abstaining from social gatherings of more than 50 people
4. Nonessential travel can resume

Phase 3

Yellow Zone
Objective
More restrictions lifted if no evidence of a rebound demonstrated
Guidelines for Individuals
1. Vulnerable individuals can resume public interactions
2. Aviod transmission through social distancing at hot spots
3. Social gathering restictions lifted as cities reopen
4. Employees can resume normal work operations

Phase 4

Green Zone
Objective
Vaccine or Treatment is available and is distributed
Guidelines for Individuals
1. Transmission drops to new normal for state or country.
2. All conditions are lifted, exception of facial masks when symptoms surface
3. New health and safety practices remaining part of daily life
4. Reopening of ALL businesses and activities
