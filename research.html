Coronavirus Treatment and Vaccine Tracker

ü¶† This page contains a cluster of publicly available information from validated sources. CovidMarkets is not endorsing one treatment over another but simply trying to spread awareness of treatments that are in development.

As know, the World Health Organization classified the COVID-19 outbreak as a pandemic. This infectious respiratory disease has reached every corner of the world, infecting millions and killing thousands.

As of April 2020, there are no FDA-approved therapies or vaccines for Covid-19. Experts and infectious disease specialists estimate there is a 12-18 month development period for the vaccine to be ready for the market.

This tracker will be updated weekly. If you notice an issue with this data or wish to submit an update, please reach out to Team


Treatments

Phase 1
Exploratory Stage
Laboratory Studies | 1-3 Months
Basic Criteria:
This stage involves laboratory research where scientists identify information relevant to the vaccine development of Coronavirus.
Identification:
Identification of natural or synthetic antigens that might help prevent or treat the virus.
Phase 2
Pre-Clinical Stage
Vaccine Concept | 3-6 months
Basic Criteria:
Small trails to identify whether the vaccine is safe for humans and what immune response it evokes. Most vaccines in trial never progress beyond this stage since they fail to produce a desired immune response.
Identification:
The creation of the vaccine concept, by the evaluation of vaccine efficacy on test subjects.
